## Email 3: How I Find 5x ROI SEO Plays

**Send timing:** Day 3
**Subject line:** The only SEO metric that matters
**Preview text:** Cost to acquire traffic vs. value of that traffic. Everything else is noise.

### PASAIDA Element: Solution

---

Forget rankings. Forget domain authority. Forget traffic volume.

One question matters: Is the spread between acquisition cost and monetization value wide enough to justify the play?

The framework:

**Full-cost acquisition modeling.** Sum every cost: content production, link building, tool allocation, time, opportunity cost of delay. Divide by projected monthly traffic at 6 months post-publish. That's cost per visitor.

**Monetization value modeling.** Calculate revenue per visitor across all sources: ads, affiliates, leads, product sales. Include LTV if you're capturing emails. Multiply by traffic projection. That's value per visitor.

**Spread calculation.** Value per visitor divided by cost per visitor equals spread multiple. Below 3x is marginal. 3-5x is solid. 5x+ prints money.

**Risk adjustment.** Multiply spread by probability of ranking (based on competition, domain authority, niche saturation). Multiply by traffic sustainability (algorithm risk, seasonality, content decay). That's risk-adjusted spread.

I only pursue plays above 3x risk-adjusted spread. This filters out 80% of opportunities. The remaining 20% drive 95% of profit.

You can do this in a spreadsheet. Takes 10 minutes per opportunity. Will change how you select keywords, niches, and content investments.
